# T2 Brief: Dust Evolution & Planetesimal Formation Simulations

## Goal
Survey the current state of dust evolution simulations and planetesimal formation models. Write findings to `outputs/.drafts/planet-formation-simulations-state-research-T2.md`.

## Key Topics to Cover

1. **Dust growth and evolution codes:** DustPy (Stammler & Birnstiel 2022), two-population models (Birnstiel et al. 2012), FARGO with dust modules, LA-COMPASS. How do they handle growth, fragmentation, radial drift, vertical settling?

2. **Pebble flux and pebble accretion:** How is the pebble mass flux computed and used as input for planet formation? Connection between dust evolution and pebble accretion efficiency (Lambrechts & Johansen 2012, 2014). Recent updates.

3. **Streaming instability and planetesimal formation:** Current understanding of SI triggering conditions (dust-to-gas ratio thresholds, particle sizes). Recent simulations (ATHENA, Pencil Code). How are planetesimal initial mass functions derived? Key papers: Johansen et al., Simon et al., Li et al.

4. **Connecting dust evolution to N-body initial conditions:** How do planetesimal formation models provide starting conditions for N-body? What are the gaps? Spatial and temporal distribution of planetesimals from SI.

5. **Recent observational constraints from ALMA:** Dust rings, gaps, substructures — how do they constrain dust evolution models? Implications for planet formation.
